Fundraising Strategy
  • Fundraisers can be held outside of lunch hours.

Professional Organizations
The Technology Association of Iowa and Business Professionals of America were discussed for event participation. Participation would require membership fees and organized travel.
Scheduling & Logistics

  • Ideas include setting up tables at events or seeking local business donations.
