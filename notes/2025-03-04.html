Date: Tuesday, March 4th, 2025

Time: 3:08 PM

Attendance: Seth Staydahar, Samantha Frasher, Dante Sims, Kyle Graves

Topics Discussed:
Fundraiser Brainstorming
  • The primary proposal is a coffee and donuts fundraiser targeted at morning campus traffic.
  • Secondary ideas include afternoon ice cream sales and IT help services for the community.
  • The club discussed reaching out to local businesses for grants and staff/faculty for donations.
Raffle Structure
  • Proposed top prizes include a 40" TV, laptops/Chromebooks, and Indian Hills gear baskets.
  • The winner selection process will allow winners to choose prizes in order of their placement.
  • Donations are expected from the IT department and potentially the campus bookstore.
Additional Concepts & Marketing
  • Other concepts discussed: Naming contests, student care packages, gaming tournaments, and movie nights.
  • Umbrella sales were suggested but ultimately declined.
  • Social media will be used to promote visibility for all upcoming fundraisers.
Motion Passed:
The club officially approved the coffee and donuts fundraiser, with the date to be determined once finer details are finalized.
