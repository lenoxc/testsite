Date: Tuesday, March 11th, 2025

Time: 3:05 PM

Attendance: Samantha Frasher, Dante Sims, Kyle Graves

Topics Discussed:
Donut Source and Pricing
  • Main Street Donuts was selected as the vendor at a rate of $15 per dozen.
  • The estimated cost per individual donut is $1.25.
  • The proposed selling price is $3 per donut or $5 for a coffee and donut bundle.
Coffee Supply & Logistics
  • Great Value (Walmart) brand coffee was selected, costing approximately $13 per container.
  • One container is estimated to produce roughly 100 cups of coffee.
  • Supplies will include filters, napkins, and cups to complete the bundle.
Profit Estimates & Inventory
The club expects a profit margin of $1.75 or more per donut when sold as part of a bundle. The initial sales goal is to move 48 donuts and 100 cups of coffee.
Fundraiser Setup
  • The event will begin at 7:00 AM to capture early teacher traffic.
  • Sam has already purchased 100 cups, with the ability to acquire more if demand exceeds expectations.
  • The team discussed balancing stock levels for this inaugural event to minimize waste.
Motion Passed:
Approved the purchase of initial supplies and the $5 bundle pricing model.
